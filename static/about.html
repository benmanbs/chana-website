Welcome to Songbird.Studio!

I’m Grace, and I have been the proud teacher of songbirds all over the world for over a decade. Teaching singing and getting my singers to realize their vocal ambitions is what inspires me most. I always look forward to that look on your face during our sessions when songbirds will say: “Oh my god, I can’t believe I sang that note!” or “I never thought it would be so easy!” - Your success is my success and I love working with you to find the sound you want.

I teach singers of all levels, ages, and genres. From round classical sounds, to hard rock distortion, to gospel growls, and country vocal breaks - all sounds are created equal in our studio and can be produced in a healthy way. When you’re in my studio - ain’t no such thing as ‘Can’t’. We will tackle all challenges with good times and laughter. After all, we say “play” music and not “work miserably hard” at music. Let’s play :)

I have over a decade of experience in teaching voice and I also have plenty of experience with vocal damage repair, improving speech impediments with vocal technique principles, and the like. I am currently a CVT (Complete Vocal Technique) teacher-in-training at Complete Vocal Institute in Copenhagen, Denmark. This method is designed to do what I love best - getting fast results, and using concrete tools that have been backed by published scientific research. Before enrolling in the teacher’s course I would coach my songbirds by using a mixture of traditional methods and modern scientific techniques that also produced fast and thorough results, and I even had a two year stint at a music conservatory program studying vocal performance in Jazz and Contemporary Music, before moving to NYC to record my first original album.

In conclusion: Whether you’re looking for better singing technique or for artistic development coaching - I’m down to get your singing journey on the fast track!
