Services
Private 1-on-1 sessions - 1 hour
(Price: $75)
These sessions are appropriate for all levels, and they provide a great focus on your particular needs and wants. It’s a great way to tackle whatever personal vocal challenges, or ambitions, you might have. If you bring the music you’d like to work with, I will bring the tools to get you the sounds you want!
Private 1-on-1 Skype sessions - 1 hour
(Price: $75)
These sessions are a perfect modern day solution for my out-of-town songbirds! They too are suitable for songbirds of all levels who might live too far away from the Songbird.Studio, but still wish to train their voices with me. Just like in the in-person sessions, you are required bring songs or sounds that you’d like to work on. These sessions require that you have a computer with a working microphone and speakers/headphones and that you make sure that you have fast and reliable internet access.
5 Private 1-on-1 sessions - 1 hour each
(Price: $350)
This is a fantastic option for songbirds who would like to have a more intensive session schedule (e.g. Bands going on tour, preparation for auditions, training for a musical show, or in studio coaching for recording artists etc.). Be aware that this package has a time limit of 5 weeks, after which the sessions expire and you’ll need to pay full price for the following lesson (or purchase the package again). If you don’t think you can commit to this schedule - I suggest you stick to single 1-on-1 sessions and wait for time to free up a bit.
